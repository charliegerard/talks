What is rapid prototyping?

The process of quickly mocking up the future state of a system

Process:

  • Prototype
  • Review
  • Refine

Why is it good?

  • Feedback early and often
  • Validate idea or raise concerns
  • Improves the design of the final product.
  • Process can be applied to anything
